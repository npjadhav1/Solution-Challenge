About Us

Poverty remains a harsh reality for millions of people around the world. Poor people often struggle to meet their basic needs, including food, shelter, healthcare, and education. They face constant insecurity, lack of opportunities, and limited access to essential resources. Children from poor families often drop out of school to support their families, which perpetuates the cycle of poverty. Unemployment and low wages make it difficult for poor people to escape their financial hardships. Empowering the poor through education and employment opportunities is crucial to improving their quality of life and ensuring a more equal and just society.
